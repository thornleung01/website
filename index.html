Who We Are

THE CAGE,
REIMAGINED

BASES LOADED is a fully automated baseball simulator facility. Four open bays. Real-time swing analytics. Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Book online, walk in, and start hitting.

We built this for the player who wants reps on their own schedule. No lessons required, no staff in the way. Just consistent pitches, honest data, and the space to get better.

The Experience

HOW IT WORKS

Open 24/7

Any hour, any day, all year. Early morning session before work or a 2am grind. We never close.

Automated Pitching

Consistent, repeatable pitches at the speed you choose. No waiting for someone to throw. Just step in and start swinging.

4 Open Bays

Four bays ready to go at all times. Come alone or bring a few friends. Plenty of space to get your work in.

Book Online, Walk In

Reserve your bay from your phone. Show up, scan in, and the system handles the rest. No front desk. No friction.

Gear Provided

Coaching

WANT A COACH?
WE'VE GOT THEM.

The bays are self-serve by default. But when you want expert eyes on your swing, our coaches bring the data to life.

1-on-1 Lessons

A coach, a bay, and your swing data on screen. Work through mechanics, timing, and approach with undivided attention.

Small Group Sessions

Bring 2–4 teammates. Rotate through the bay, compare numbers, and push each other. Coached competition.

Swing Analysis

Sit down with a coach and break down your session data. Frame-by-frame video, exit velo trends, and a clear plan forward.

Season Prep

Multi-week programs built around your tryout or season timeline. Structured progressions with measurable benchmarks.

Youth Programs

Age-appropriate coaching that builds real fundamentals. Kids learn proper mechanics early, before bad habits set in.

Competitive Track

For players chasing roster spots. Advanced programming with performance targets, progress tracking, and accountability.
